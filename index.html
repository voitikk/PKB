Want to Know More About Us?

We are an online service that conducts in-depth baseball analysis on video sent in by players of all ages. Our coaching staff, made up only of current professional baseball players, work hard to provide our clients with a totally unique, constructive, and convenient coaching experience. Our system is designed to equip young baseball players and their parents with a structured and informative analysis of video sent in by you! In addition to a written analysis of the video, our coaches provide you with videos of themselves performing drills specific to improving your player's skill set. The written analysis along with the videos are tools we encourage you store on your phone and computer to be used as constant reminders of how your child can best evolve as a baseball player.

How Do We Do It?

Our Pros use a video analysis application called Hudl Technique to analyze the film our clients send in. This application allows for our Pros to breakdown the player's mechanics and diagnose the flaws preventing him from reaching his full potential. Our initial analysis of a player extensively covers the areas the player must improve in, how to make those improvements, and how to strengthen the things the player already does well. After the initial analysis, we recommend players receive follow up analyses once a month. We have included examples of both an initial and a follow-up evaluation below.
