Follow-Up Analyses

A Follow-Up Analysis should be requested by all clients who have already received the Initial Analysis. We recommend that athletes send in a new video and request a Follow-Up Analysis once every month. A new analysis each month allows for your athlete to receive new coaching, drills, and professional tips, which will help him continue to make improvements on top of the ones he made in the prior month. A monthly analysis also allows for our Pros to keep track of the progress the athlete is making over time and identify which drills and strategies produced the best results.
