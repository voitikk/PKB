Pros Know Best

Here's how to get started

Step 1

Use your iPhone, iPad, Samsung, or other mobile device/ tablet to record the player either pitching, hitting or fielding

Tips for the "Ideal Video" are listed below! You may also use a Flip camera.

Step 2

Then, either a parent or the athlete must send an email to us requesting a full analysis for ONE of the following: Pitching, Hitting, or Fielding

Email instructions are listed below, underneath the "Ideal Video" notes.

Step 3

After your request is processed and passed onto one of our Pros, a member of our team will inform you as to which Pro is handling your analysis and will also provide you with payment instructions

All payment will be accepted via PayPal. You can create an account in less than 5 minutes at PayPal.com, if you do not already have one.

Step 4

Upon completion of your analysis, you will receive an email containing each part of the analysis package, which includes written, video and photo components


Check out the "Completed Analysis (Example)" near the bottom of our home page to know what to expect. Also, please allow 1-3 business days for your analysis to be completed.

Step 5

What comes next is determined by you! Our analysis and drills CAN and SHOULD be used on a daily basis to ensure maximum development

We encourage our clients to take advantage of our professional development service once a month. This allows the athlete to have enough time to train and evolve as a baseball player using our previous instruction before being reevaluated by our staff

The Ideal Video

Tips on recording the ideal video

  • 1. Film the player performing their windup, swing, or fielding motion during a controlled practice environment (live game footage is acceptable but is more difficult to analyze)
  • 2. Film 3-5 repetitions of the motion - videos should be approximately 15-30 seconds long
  • 3. Film the player from 2 or 3 angles - this allows our professionals to analyze every moving piece during the motion
  • 4. Film the video on an iPhone, iPad, Samsung, or other mobile device
